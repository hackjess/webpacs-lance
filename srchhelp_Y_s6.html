Search Tips Examples
PHRASE Use “quotes” to search a phrase. "college students"
"united states supreme court"
TRUNCATION Use * to substitute for 1-5 characters.
Use ** to substitute for any number of characters.
Use ? to substitute for a single character. 		photograph*
math**
wom?n
CONNECTORS Use and to search all words.
Use or to search ANY word.
Use and not to exclude words.
Use parentheses to group terms for complex searches. 		environment and health
treatment or therapy
dolphins and not football
(doctor* or physician*) and stress
PROXIMITY Use within and a number to specify proximity of words, one to another, in any order. missouri within 2 university
FIELDS Use labels to search specific fields:
a: author
t: title
s: subject 		a:shakespeare and t:lear
s:hopi and s:religion
