MICROMATERIALS

  Location:         Periodicals/Microforms Area.
                          First floor north (west side of atrium).

                          Microfilm is shelved in LC call number order.

                          Microfiche is shelved in cabinets by cabinet and drawer number.

                          MicroNewspapers do not have call numbers.  Please ask for assistance at the Library Service Desk.

  Loan period:   Micromaterials are to be used in the library.
                         You may make photocopies; cost is 10 cents per page.
                         You may also download to disc; inquire at the Library Service Desk.
